What is Safe Living?

Safe Living is an all-in-one tool designed for those looking to move to find safe cities. The Safe Living website incorporates information on crime from the FBI to rank cities' safety!

How is the Safe Living Score calculated?

The "Safe Living Score" is our safety score for a city! The score is a rank of the safety of each city compared to the entire United States. For example, a Safe Living Score of 50 means the city is safer than 50% of the cities in the United States.

Can I get a Safe Living Score for cities outside of the US?

Unfortunately, our Safe Living Score is only available for cities in the United States. The Safe Living Score works for every city with valid crime data in the United States!
